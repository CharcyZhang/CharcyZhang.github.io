Definition to Friendship

Establishing friendships had always been an essential part of daily life. However, due to the development of a competitive world, being friends and making friends had became harder and harder. There are three different types of friendships, including: utility friendships, friendship of pleasure and friendship of virtue. The friendship of virtue is often regarded as the most ideal type of friendship since it is a relationship regardless of profit or gains. This blog will state the reasons to maintain virtue friendships and suggest methods to build such kinds of relationships.

The reason that people should maintain virtue friendships is because of its preciousness. Virtue friendships are the sacrifice not considering the gains, the accompany and advice in all situations no matter loss or gain and the similar values or characters. Unlike utility friends and pleasure friends, virtue friends do not focus on what they can get from the relationship, it is the friendship itself that benefits both of them, suiting them into better person. Additionally, virtue friendships have positive effects on people’s well-being. Virtue friendships is the unconditional love toward each other. Scientific research shows that the meaningful social interactions between virtue friendships lead to a healthy lifestyles and a longer life span. What’s more, this beneficial relationship also shows help in mental aspect. The feeling of being needed and loved creates a optimistic mood and increases the serotonin and dopamine level of people.

Because of its importance, it is vital to maintain virtue friendships with others. Firstly, communication and updates are keys to keep virtue friendships with others. The familiarity to each others life allows advice and comments to be given. Discuss what is meaningful and what is trivial, the similarity in values and characters smooths the discussion. Consistency is always the most essential part of maintaining virtue friendships. Furthermore, the balance in help and vulnerability also boosts the relationship. Helping and receiving help strengthen the relationships, people has the feeling of being needed and experiences the joy and pleasure of providing support to others. In the process, it is worth to mention that the receiver must be willing to accept the support with grace and gratitude, otherwise the help will be understood as the compassion to the weaker side.

Maintaining such kinds of beneficial relationship is never a easy case. Life is constantly changing, and people can experience different events. It is never possible for two people to feel exactly same toward the issues.These different views and experiences can have a profound effect on relationships and may start to hinder the very relationships that values as being important. In order to solve this problem, it is essential to stay present in the friends’ life. When encountering obstacles or conflicts in values, it is not worth to debate and see who is correct but to give advice and be conscious of the attitudes and behaviors. Staying with each others, and together push through the difficulties is the permanent shared aims between virtue friends.
To summarize, the essay explores different types of friendships and proposes of making virtue friends and suggests the benefits and ways to maintain virtue friendships.
